The last piece of advice I gave someone was on Romance and specifically finding a partner. My advice to my friend was that it is highly unlikely to find the perfect partner for most people. So, when he comes across different partner options in his life, he should consider if he has reached a point in knowing enough about a person and that if some new negative thing comes to knowledge, he would still love his partner.
